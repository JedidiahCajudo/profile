About

I’m a web developer bringing businesses into the modern era with smooth and responsive web applications. I design the user interface, set up the backend, and deploy it online. I am most passionate about building something that works well and looks appealing.

I'm passionate about businesses who are inclusive and empathetic to all types of people.

Based in Tokyo, Japan, I’m usually taking photos around the city or getting a ramen or coffee.
